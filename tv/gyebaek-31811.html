Gye Baek, Warrior’s Fate

Gyebaek is a 2011 South Korean historical television drama series, starring Lee Seo-jin, Jo Jae-hyun, Oh Yeon-soo, Song Ji-hyo and Hyomin. It aired on Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation from July 23 to November 22, 2011 on Mondays and Tuesdays at 21:55 for 32 episodes. Set in the Baekje kingdom in the mid-7th century, the drama chronicles the life and times of the storied warrior General Gyebaek who is remembered in history for leading Baekje's last stand against the Silla in the Battle of Hwangsanbeol.
